Project

I was inspired to do this project on how to make peanutbutter and jelly sandwiches because I love food, and PB&J sandwiches have always had an infleunce on me growing up. I find that with most people, PB&J sandwiches have incorporated themselves into people's lives at some point. While some may think its not difficult to screw up a PB&J, you'd be very surprised. Although, the beauty behind the art of making these sandwiches is through personal preferences and tastes. One can make this sandwhich very unique based on what they enjoy or what kind of mood they are in. Whether that may be changing the ratio of PB to jelly; incorporating Nutella or honey; and even the simple extra step to toast the bread beforehand; making a peanutbutter and jelly sandwhich can be an artistic masterpiecee.
