How to Make a Peanutbutter & Jelly Sandwhich

Introduction

Hello you eager and hungry people! This site is dedicated to help instruct you on how to make the BEST peanutbutter and jelly sandwhich in the world!

Materials Needed

  1. Peanutbutter: highly recommended to use Jiff
  2. Jelly: highly recommended to use Smuckers
  3. Bread: any brand bread will do
  4. 1 Plate
  5. 2 Knives

Instructions

First Step

  1. Layout materials and place 2 slices of bread on the plate.

Second Step

  1. Take one of the knives and apply the peanutbutter evenly across one of the slices of bread. As shown above. The quantity used is based on personal preference.

Third Step

  1. Take the other knife and apply the jelly evenly across the other slice of bread. As shown above. The quantity used is based on personal preference.

Final Step

  1. Finally, take the knife you just used and either cut the sandwhich horizontally or diagonally. As shown below.


    2. *This final step is optional. The sandwhich is just as enjoyable in its full form.

    **Quick note, if you are not a fan of crust on your sandwhich; cut the sandwhich diagonally. This will create less crust and more PBJ Surface Area per bite.
